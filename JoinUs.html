We are looking for oustanding candidates. If you are interested in joining the lab, please contact us. We will post here any internal job openings immediately available.
   
   
   

For Post-docs

There are several prestigious fellowships that open annually. If you are interested in joining us, please have a look at:

Marie Curie Fellowships, Newton International Fellowships, Leverhulme Research Fellowship, Imperial College Junior Research Fellowships, EPSRC Postdoctoral Fellowship and Royal Academy of Engineering Research Fellowship
   
   
   

For Ph.D Students

If you are a home student (UK citizenship or EU who has stayed in UK for a certain time period) and have a good UG background, there might be a good chance to earn a scholarship. Overseas student candidates are highly encouraged to apply to appropriate scholarships in their home countries or in the college. Please look here to identify appropriate schemes for you. For Chinese student candidates, please have a look at CSC-Imperial scholarships. Also refer to Joint PhD Scholarships with HKU and NUS. There seem quite programmes available within EEE department for outstanding student candidates of any nationality. Please contact us if you want to see into the above. Note we receive a large number of student applications and we cannot respond to all enquiries promptly.
