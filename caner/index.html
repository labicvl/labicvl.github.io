I am a fourth-year PhD student in the Imperial Computer Vision and Learning Lab, at the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering of Imperial College London. With the supervision of Dr. Tae-Kyun Kim, my research topic is 6 DoF object detection and pose estimation.

I got master degree in Mechatronics Engineering from Sabanci University, Istanbul, Turkey, under the supervision of Prof. Mustafa Unel. I am a double in Electronic & Communication Engineering and in Mechanical Engineering, both majors are from Yildiz Tech, Istanbul, Turkey.
