I am a PhD student in the Imperial Computer Vision and Learning Lab, at the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering of Imperial College London. With the supervision of Dr. Tae-Kyun Kim, my research topic is 6 DoF object detection and pose estimation.

I got my MSc degree in Mechatronics Engineering from Sabanci University, Istanbul, Turkey in 2013, under the supervision of Prof. Mustafa Unel.

I received double major BSc degrees in Electronic & Communication Engineering and Mechanical Engineering-with a 1st position (GPA: 3.78/4) from Yildiz Technical University, Istanbul, Turkey in 2012 and 2010, respectively.
