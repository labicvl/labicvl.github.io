Call for Papers

  • All submissions will be handled electronically via the MSR CMT system
  • As with the CVPR main conference, papers should be 6 to 8 pages (excluding citations).
  • Submissions will be subjected to a double blind peer review process. Authors should guarantee that a substantially similar work has not been previously published nor is under review.
  • Submissions should use the CVPR 2016 word or LaTex templates tar or zip
  • Authors will be required to transfer, to the IEEE, copyrights for any papers published in the conference proceedings.

Call for Extended Abstracts

  • In addition to regular papers, we also invite extended abstracts of ongoing or published work. We see this as an opportunity for authors to promote their work to an interested audience.
  • Extended abstracts are limited to three pages.
  • The submission must be sent to cvpr.hands.2016@gmail.com.

Call for Participation in the Challenge

We also invite authors of previous and ongoing work to submit empirical results to our challenge. Such results will be presented at the workshop and likely cited in later work.
